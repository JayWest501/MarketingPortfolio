Project Context

This project was developed independently as a practical application of HubSpot email marketing principles. The scenario: a B2B SaaS product seeing 63% of trial users go inactive between days 5–9. Existing lifecycle emails in the scenario had a 14% open rate and no behavioral triggers — every user received the same sequence regardless of activity level. My goal was to redesign the entire flow from scratch.

Strategy & Execution

1
Audited HubSpot workflow logic and identified 3 conflicting enrollment triggers canceling each other out
2
Built behavioral segmentation: Active, Dormant, Feature-Stuck, and Value-Unclear cohorts
3
Rewrote all 5 email templates with persona-matched copy and single CTAs per touch
4
A/B tested subject lines across 3 segments; implemented winning variants at day 21
5
Set up engagement scoring to hand off warm leads to sales at threshold 65+

Project Context

Built as an MBA marketing strategy project. The scenario: a growing HR tech company producing content reactively with no editorial calendar, no repurposing system, and a two-person team creating isolated one-off pieces. LinkedIn wasn't tied to blog content. Emails weren't tied to campaigns. I was tasked with designing a scalable content operation from the ground up.

Strategy & Execution

1
Audited 6 months of existing content; identified 3 themes with strongest engagement signals
2
Designed a content pyramid: 1 pillar → 3 short-form → 5 social → 3 email → 2 video scripts
3
Built a 90-day editorial calendar in Notion with ownership, deadlines, and repurposing instructions for each asset
4
Created style guide + content brief templates so writers could self-serve without senior oversight on every piece
5
Tracked performance in a shared dashboard — top posts fed back into next quarter's pillar topics

HubSpot CRM Overhaul — B2B Revenue Operations

Project Context

Developed independently to demonstrate practical HubSpot CRM and marketing operations skills. The scenario: a 40-person B2B professional services company with 12,400 contacts in HubSpot — 38% duplicates, 22% with no lifecycle stage, and no scoring system in place. Sales and marketing were operating from different definitions of a qualified lead. I designed the full remediation plan as a standalone deliverable.

Strategy & Execution

1
Exported full contact list; ran dedup audit and merged 4,700 records across 6 company domains
2
Facilitated a sales + marketing alignment session to agree on MQL / SQL definitions and handoff SLAs
3
Built HubSpot lead scoring model: 35 behavioral signals (email opens, page views, downloads) weighted by intent
4
Created automated lifecycle stage transitions and internal notifications for sales at score threshold 60
5
Built a custom HubSpot dashboard tracking pipeline health, MQL velocity, and sales accepted lead rate weekly
